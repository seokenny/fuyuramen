About Fuyu Ramen

Established in Sunnyside Queens, Fuyu Ramen serves a variety of dishes from Japanese and Korean cuisine. From appetizers to ramen to rice bowls, Fuyu Ramen offers authentic and fusion dishes. With a wide selection of alcohol drinks local and imported, customers can enjoy new and exotic drinks that they've never had before. The restaurant has both tables, communal seatings, and bar seats.
