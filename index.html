About Fuyu Ramen

Established in Sunnyside Queens, Fuyu Ramen serves a variety of dishes including Japanese and Korean cuisine. From appetizers, entrees, ramen, rice bowls, and beverages, Fuyu Ramen has food that will fit the taste of everyone. The shop is run by two Korean friends who attended a culinary class that brought them the reputation to create such delicious food.
